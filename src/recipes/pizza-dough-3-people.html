Makes 2 medium pizzas or 1 large + 1 small. Perfect for 3 people.

Ingredients

Add to bread maker in this exact order:

  • 230ml water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (about 24g)
  • 380g strong white bread flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dry yeast (instant/active dry)

Method

  1. Add ingredients to bread maker in the order listed
  2. Select option 26 (pizza dough)
  3. Takes about 45 minutes
  4. Remove dough and place on floured surface
  5. Divide into 2 balls (or 1 large + 1 small)
  6. Cover and let rest 10-15 minutes
  7. Shape by hand or rolling pin

Baking

  • Preheat oven to 250°C (or highest setting)
  • Use pizza stone or baking tray
  • Bake 8-12 minutes depending on toppings

Bread maker: Panasonic SD-YR2550SXC
